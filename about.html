Project Mission

This dashboard was created to provide the Bellbrook community with a simple, mobile-first way to check bridge accessibility during rain events.

By automating the data from the river gauges, we remove the guesswork of calculating clearance manually from height tables.

Bridge Reference

Deck Height 2.80m
Source Gauge Bellbrook

Clearance is calculated as:
2.80m (Deck) - River Height = Clearance

Note: Local debris or wave action can affect safety even if the data shows minor clearance.
