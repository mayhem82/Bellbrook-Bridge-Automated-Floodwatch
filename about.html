Floodwatch — Bellbrook

A missing layer between raw data and real decisions.

Mission

Floodwatch provides a real-time answer to: Can I safely cross the bridge right now?

By showing live heights and clearance, it removes the need for rumors or guessing during flood events.

Intentional Limits

Floodwatch shows only what is true now. It does not forecast or model scenarios. Each measurement is transparent and derived from public gauge data.

Core Principle: Withholding simple, actionable information does not increase safety. Providing it—clearly and without interpretation—does.
