The Mission

This project exists for one reason: to give the Bellbrook community a clear, immediate answer to "Can I cross the bridge?" during high-water events.

While official data exists, it is often buried in tables or requires manual calculation. This tool automates that process to save time when it matters most.

My Journey Building It

Living in an area where the bridge is our lifeline, I realized that checking river heights was a repetitive chore for every family in the village. I started by tracking the gauge data manually, but soon realized I could build a "bridge-first" dashboard.

The journey involved teaching myself how to fetch live data from BOM and Council feeds, building a system to remember the history of the river (the trend graph), and designing a mobile-friendly interface that works even when phone reception is poor during a storm.

This is a labor of love for Bellbrook—built by a resident, for the residents.
